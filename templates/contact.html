Frequently Asked Questions

Find answers to common questions

What documents do I need to rent a car?

You'll need a valid driver's license, passport or ID, and a credit card for the security deposit. International visitors may also need an International Driving Permit.

What's included in the rental price?

Our rental prices include basic insurance, unlimited mileage, and 24/7 roadside assistance. Additional services like GPS and extra drivers are available for an extra fee.

Can I cancel my booking?

Yes, you can cancel your booking free of charge up to 24 hours before your rental date. Cancellations within 24 hours may incur a fee.

What's your fuel policy?

We provide the vehicle with a full tank and expect it to be returned with a full tank. If returned with less fuel, you'll be charged for the difference at current market rates.

Do you offer airport delivery?

Yes, we offer convenient airport pickup and drop-off services. We can arrange to meet you at the airport with your rental vehicle ready.

What if I have an accident?

Contact us immediately at +250 788 616 618. Our team will guide you through the process. All vehicles are insured, and we'll handle the claim.
