About Fido Car Rental

Your trusted partner in premium car rental services

Who We Are

Fido Car Rental & General Supply is a leading car rental company serving East Africa with a commitment to excellence, reliability, and customer satisfaction. Founded with a vision to provide premium vehicle rental services, we have grown to become a trusted name in the industry.

With years of experience in the automotive and hospitality sectors, we understand the unique needs of travelers and businesses. Our fleet of well-maintained vehicles and professional team ensure that every journey is comfortable, safe, and memorable.

Whether you're exploring the natural beauty of Rwanda, conducting business, or planning a special event, Fido Car Rental is your ideal partner.

Our Mission & Vision

Driving excellence in car rental services

Our Mission

To provide exceptional car rental services that exceed customer expectations through reliable vehicles, professional service, and competitive pricing. We are committed to making travel convenient, comfortable, and affordable for everyone.

Our Vision

To be the leading car rental company in East Africa, recognized for our commitment to quality, innovation, and customer satisfaction. We aspire to expand our services and continue building long-term relationships with our clients.
